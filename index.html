My Vision with My Million

BOOKS (₱ 25,000)

×

My passion for reading, particularly in the field of psychology, is driven by an insatiable curiosity about human behavior and cognition. This intellectual pursuit extends to self-reflection, where I eagerly investigate my own actions. When I notice a specific behavioral pattern, I delve into literature that delves into the root causes, assessing whether it's beneficial or detrimental. In instances of negative behaviors, I search for strategies to overcome these challenges. Furthermore, I appreciate the narratives shared in the books I peruse, particularly when authors interweave their personal experiences. Learning about their unique viewpoints and insights is a captivating aspect of my reading habit. Lastly, for entertainment purpose, I also wanted to buy a lot of manga books.
